# 🏋️ FitForge – Personalized Gym Companion

[![Vite](https://img.shields.io/badge/Vite-B73BFE?style=for-the-badge&logo=vite&logoColor=FFD62E)](https://vitejs.dev/)

[![Framer Motion](https://img.shields.io/badge/Framer_Motion-black?style=for-the-badge&logo=framer&logoColor=white)](https://www.framer.com/motion/)

FitForge is a premium, mobile-first web application designed to be your ultimate workout partner. It generates **daily personalized routines** based on your unique body data, fitness goals, and progress trends.

> [!TIP]
> **Built for Consistency**: FitForge uses a smart logic engine to adapt your difficulty levels as you grow, ensuring you never hit a plateau.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧠 Smart Workout Generator
- **Goal-Based Splits**: Tailored routines for *Weight Loss, Muscle Gain, Strength,* and *General Fitness*.
- **Adaptive Difficulty**: Difficulty scales (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced) based on your profile.
- **7-Day Intelligence**: Automatically rotates exercises across your weekly schedule.

### 📅 Daily Dashboard
- **Forge Ahead**: See "Today's Routine" at a glance.
- **Micro-Tracking**: Easily view sets, reps, and rest times for every exercise.
- **Streak Tracking 🔥**: Keep your momentum alive with visual daily check-ins.

### 🏋️ Exercise Library
- **50+ Categorized Exercises**: Deep dive into Chest, Back, Legs, Core, and more.
- **Smart Filter & Search**: Find exactly what you need in seconds with real-time filtering.

### 📈 Progress & Insights
- **Body Metrics**: Track your weight trends over time with interactive charts.
- **History Log**: Revisit your past wins and workout logs anytime.

### 🎨 Premium Experience
- **Futuristic Aesthetics**: Sleek dark mode with glassmorphism effects.
- **Theming**: Toggle between Dark and Light modes on the fly.
- **Smooth Animations**: Powered by Framer Motion for a state-of-the-art feel.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Core**: [React 19](https://react.dev/) + [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/)
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS (Modern Design System)
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
- **State Management**: React Context API
- **Persistence**: Web Storage (LocalStorage)

## 📜 Design Philosophy

FitForge is built on the principle of **"Frictionless Fitness."** By removing the guesswork from "what should I do today?", we empower users to focus entirely on their performance. The UI is designed to minimize distractions while providing high visual feedback on achievements.
